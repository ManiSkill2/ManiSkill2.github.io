Diverse Objects


ManiSkill2 features 20 out-of-the-box task families with 2,000+ objects in total for task variations.

You don't have to collect assets or define tasks by yourself, and can focus on your methods.

Large-Scale Demonstrations


We verify that each task in ManiSkill2 is solvable, and collect 4,000,000+ frames of demonstrations in total.

You can focus on many interesting aspects of the problem (e.g., network designs and learning algorithms), instead of struggling with data collection.

Fast Visual RL Support


Our visual RL speed is faster than all mainstream robot leanring benchmarks, with our asynchronous render system.

So, you can study visual RL algorithms without worrying too much about the rendering burden.

3D Point Cloud Support


We supporet 3D point cloud observations natively.

Feel free to apply your favoriate 3D point cloud methods!
